# Pixel Art Image Prompts

**Style directive for ALL prompts**: Pixel art style, 128-bit / 256-pixel aesthetic, warm colors, retro adventure game feel. Featuring Haku: a cute orange-and-white fluffy pixel cat. NOT photorealistic, NOT flat vector, NOT 3D render. Think cozy retro game sprite art.
